WELCOME TO YNWA

About YNWA

Our Mission

We help individuals who are overcoming difficulties.
We understand how helpful it is for people going through life issues
to find a space where they can build connections
with others who understand what they're experiencing


What we offer

As a member of our community, you have a number of ways to connect with people with similar experiences
Each option offers one the opportunity to make their own contribution


Community Forums

Our community forums provide the kind of
comprehensive support you seek as you navigate your challenges.
Our support group wants to feel comfortable and at ease


Peer to Peer Groups

If you’re not sure which program fits you, joining one of our Peer-to-Peer Groups
is a great place to start. Whatever you decide, keep in mind that you can
always try other options until you find the right fit for you.
Contact us if you’d like more information.
Our support group wants to feel comfortable and at ease
